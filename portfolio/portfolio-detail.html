The Work

Adamo has developed a cloud solution for mobile applications and web browsers. Doctors can visit premature babies through a mobile application. Thereby giving advice and timely treatment for premature babies. In addition, the doctor will save information into the medical record after each visit. Your doctor may also see reports of your child's development, immunization reports and medical facility visits. Application that helps Parents take care of babies born more easily. Get timely consultation and consultation with doctors. In addition, Parents also have access to modern scientific knowledge for newborn care.

The Challenge

As perinatal and neonatal care are advancing, more preterm babies are surviving and being discharged from neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) to be cared for by parents. Due to complicated health problems, premature babies require extended and special follow up care after discharge. However, follow up care for such high-risk babies is not available in most LMICs as Vietnam. Innovative solutions are urgently needed to ensure the optimal care and best outcomes for preterm survivors in these countries.
